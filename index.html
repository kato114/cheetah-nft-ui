About Us

The Velas Cheetah Club is a high quality hand drawn coalition within the Velas blockchain, all of the VCC artwork is stored on IPFS, the Interplanetary File System is a peer-to-peer hypermedia protocol designed to preserve your collectibles.

With over 90 assets and millions of combinations every Velas Cheetah will be unique and will have different rarity rankings based on the common, uncommon, rare and super rare assets they adopt, the higher the attribute rating, the higher your VCC will rank.
